Common questions

  • All your trip data, documents, and Safe contents are stored only on your device - we never see them.
  • The Safe is protected by Face ID (or your device passcode as a fallback).
  • Backups are encrypted with a password only you know - if you lose it, we cannot recover your backup.

See our Privacy Policy for details on how your data is handled.
