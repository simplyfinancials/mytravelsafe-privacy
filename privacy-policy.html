Privacy Policy

MyTravelSafe

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 Your documents, Safe contents, passport and card details, and trip information are stored encrypted on your device and are never collected or transmitted. To keep the app free, we show ads and collect anonymous usage analytics that contain no personal information — details below.

Data Collection

MyTravelSafe does not collect personal data that identifies you — no names, email addresses, or account details. Your trip information, documents, and files are stored exclusively and encrypted on your device and are never transmitted to us. To keep the app free we use advertising and anonymous analytics (see the Advertising and Analytics sections below); these collect only non-identifying usage and device data, never your documents or Safe contents.

Local Storage

All data — trips, tasks, documents, and Safe files — is stored locally on your device using SQLite and the device file system. Sensitive documents in the Safe are encrypted using a key stored in your device's secure Keychain. No data ever leaves your device without your explicit action (e.g. using the Backup export feature).

AI Features

If you choose to use the AI Travel Planner or AI Assistant, your messages are sent directly to Anthropic's API using an API key you provide. We never see, store, or proxy these requests. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

Backup & Restore

The Backup feature exports your data as an encrypted file that you save to a location of your choice (e.g. iCloud Drive, email). The encryption passphrase is chosen by you and never stored or transmitted by MyTravelSafe. We have no access to your backups.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics — events like "trip created" or "task completed." These events contain no personal information, names, email addresses, or device identifiers. The data is used solely to understand which features are useful and to improve the app. You can review PostHog's privacy policy at posthog.com/privacy.

Advertising

To keep MyTravelSafe free, we display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and usage information (such as a device advertising identifier, approximate location, and ad interactions) to serve and measure ads. On iOS, the first time you open the app we ask, through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, whether you allow tracking for personalized ads. If you decline, you still see ads — they are just non-personalized — and you can use the entire app. Ads never have access to your documents, Safe contents, passport or card details, or trip information. See Google's privacy policy for how AdMob handles data.

Third-Party Services

The Deals tab may display travel offers from third-party affiliate partners (for example travel insurance, eSIM, and hotel providers). Tapping a deal opens the partner's website in your browser, and we may earn a commission. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. All deal links are validated to use HTTPS before being opened.
