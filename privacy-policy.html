Privacy Policy

MyTravelSafe

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 Your documents, Safe contents, passport and card details, and trip information are stored encrypted on your device and are never collected or transmitted. We collect anonymous usage analytics that contain no personal information - details below.

Data Collection

MyTravelSafe does not collect personal data that identifies you - no names, email addresses, or account details. Your trip information, documents, and files are stored exclusively and encrypted on your device and are never transmitted to us. We use anonymous analytics (see the Analytics section below); this collects only non-identifying usage data, never your documents or Safe contents. MyTravelSafe does not currently show ads.

Local Storage

All data - trips, tasks, documents, and Safe files - is stored locally on your device using SQLite and the device file system. Sensitive documents in the Safe are encrypted using a key stored in your device's secure Keychain. No data ever leaves your device without your explicit action (e.g. using the Backup export feature).

Backup & Restore

The Backup feature exports your data as an encrypted file that you save to a location of your choice (e.g. iCloud Drive, email). The encryption passphrase is chosen by you and never stored or transmitted by MyTravelSafe. We have no access to your backups.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics - events like "deal tapped" or "backup created." These events contain no personal information, names, email addresses, or device identifiers. The data is used solely to understand which features are useful and to improve the app. You can review PostHog's privacy policy at posthog.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

The Deals tab may display travel offers from third-party affiliate partners (for example travel insurance providers). Tapping a deal opens the partner's website in your browser, and we may earn a commission. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. All deal links are validated to use HTTPS before being opened.

Contact
